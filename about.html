My Story

I'm a teacher based in Maine, with over 10 years of experience teaching math, computer science, and robotics. My journey into teaching started with a pasion create a posiive learning environment for all students. I started in math and broke into computer science and robotics in 2018, and it has grown into a career I'm truly passionate and proud about.


I currently teach a range of high school classes in both computer science and robotics. These classes range from introduction classes to AP classes. I also am the advisor and mentor for our schools VEX Robotics program, currenly hosting 5 teams.

When I'm not coding, teaching, or coaching you will find me exploring the beautiful Maine coastline, traveling domestically and abroad, reading, or spending time with friends and family.


I believe in continuous learning and always strive to improve my skills and stay current with industry best practices so that my students are learning the skills they need for success.
